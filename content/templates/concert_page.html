CYO meets Circus and Vaudeville!

The Bindlestiff Family Circus joins forces with CYO to explore the music that accompanies trapeze artists, clowns, contortionists, tightrope walkers, and all the acts you'll find in a circus. The circus is one of the few remaining performing arts groups that still tours with live musicians. This installment in this series will exemplify what it takes to be a circus musician. And it's a tough gig.

New Works

If you're looking or Mozart, you're in the wrong place. Every season CYO performs New Works and explores new composers and genres. We make Contemporary music. We learn all about it, from the composers who create it. CYO pursues this with relentless energy and excitement. New works are learned and performed every season. This makes CYO a different kind of musical experience, all about creating and learning how the music of today is created, prepared and performed. This season is no different.
